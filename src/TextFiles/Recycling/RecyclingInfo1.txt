Hvis du mener at det vil være for svært at reparere, men stadig ville genbruge elektronikken mest muligt, er der stadig en række muligheder.

- Tage det til en genbrugsplads
- Donere det
- Tilbagekøb / Buyback
~If you deem reparation too hard, but still want to recycle your electronics as best as possible, there are still some options to keep in mind.

- Take it to a recycler
- Donate it
- Buyback
