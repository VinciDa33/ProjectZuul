CPU-køleren skal aldrig monteres før CPU'en, SATA drev installers ikke på bundkortet, og GPU'en er fastsat på både bundkortet og kabinettet, derfor bør det monteres efter bundkortet. The CPU-cooler should never be mounted before the CPU, SATA drives are not installed on the motherboard, and the GPU is fastened to both the motherboard and PC case, therefore it should be mounted after the motherboard is inserted in the case.
